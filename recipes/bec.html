Bagidakadakda (Bacon egg and cheese)

A classic deli delicacy, the bacon, egg and cheese is a staple of food culture and an iconic piece of cultural history.

Ingrediants

  • Bacon
  • egg
  • cheese
  • bread

Recipe

  1. precook bacon
  2. start cooking egg after bacon is done
  3. when egg nears the end of cooking, play cheese on top
  4. place the bacon on one side of the egg and fold the egg over in the pan
  5. pull off the pan and place on the bread
  6. plate and serve
