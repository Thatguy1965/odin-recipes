Nashville spicy chicken sandwich

This spicy sandwich recipe is a classic and a common favourite amoung the younger generations. With it's spicy and savoury flavor and the juicy chicken covered in crips breading, it's hard to see why this wouldn't be a favourite amoung spice lovers.

Ingrediants

  • 12oz chicken breast
  • seasoning selection (paprika, salt, pepper)
  • hot sauce of choice
  • sandwich bun
  • pickles

Recipe

  1. season chicken breast then apply hotsauce and breading
  2. bake chicken breast and cut pickles
  3. construct sandwich and plate
